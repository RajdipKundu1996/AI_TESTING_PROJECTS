About eMudhra

www.emudhra.com — eMudhra Limited is a leading global provider of digital identity solutions and trust services, headquartered in Bangalore, India. As a licensed Certifying Authority (CA) under the Indian IT Act and a globally recognized Trust Service Provider, eMudhra delivers cutting-edge solutions across:

  • Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) — Enterprise-grade certificate management
  • Digital Signatures — Legally binding e-signatures (DSC, eSign)
  • e-Authentication — Multi-factor identity verification
  • Enterprise Cybersecurity — Zero-trust architectures and compliance
  • IoT Security — Device identity and secure communication

eMudhra serves 900+ enterprise customers across 70+ countries, including major financial institutions, government agencies, and Fortune 500 companies. Listed on both BSE and NSE, eMudhra is committed to making the digital world more secure and trustworthy.
